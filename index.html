ACAV100M

Automatic Curation of large Audio-Visual datasets

About

We present both an automated curation pipeline and curated datasets for audio-visual self-supervised learning.

We select videos with high audio-visual correspondence by evaluating mutual information between the audio and visual features. Since our pipeline is fully automatic and does not require human verification, it is easily scalable to millions of videos.

We provide five data subsets of difference scales curated from 140M raw videos.
